About Us

Welcome to Avong Nen Romy

Located in the cool highlands of La Trinidad, Benguet, Avong Nen Romy is a nature-based destination that offers a perfect balance of relaxation and adventure. Surrounded by pine trees and fresh mountain air, it provides a peaceful escape where visitors can unwind while enjoying exciting outdoor activities.

Originally developed as a private property, it has grown into a well-known eco-tourism site that promotes wellness, recreation, and community engagement. Here, people can recharge, bond, and create lasting memories.

Our Mission

To provide a safe, exciting, and nature-centered environment where individuals and groups can enjoy outdoor activities, develop skills, and experience meaningful moments.

Our Vision

To become one of the leading outdoor recreation destinations in Benguet, known for adventure, quality service, and unforgettable experiences.

Why Choose Us

  • Unique combination of archery, throwing, and airsoft
  • Safe and controlled activity areas
  • Relaxing natural environment
  • Perfect for both adventure and leisure
  • Ideal for friends, families, and teams
